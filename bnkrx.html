About

The BNKR Extended Token captures the network value of the Bankroll DeFi Network and allows everyone in the TRON community to participate passively. BNKRX is scarce, can be staked, has progressive mining difficulty and elastic inflation which scales to the savings rate of the community.

BNKRX is harvested via Bankroll Farm. How much BNKRX you receive is based on the current mining difficulty. It's liquidity grows in proportion to its inflation rate and is locked.
