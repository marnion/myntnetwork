About

Treasury is used to passively earn rewards while providing locked liquidity. STCK is pegged 1:1 to MYNTXXSWAP, MYNTXX's native liquidity pool token. Farm contributes 25% of mined MYNTXX to Treasury players via daily drip; an additional 25% is contributed to locked liquidity.
