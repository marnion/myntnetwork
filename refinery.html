About

MYNT Refinery is the ultimate passive income stream available on MYNT Network, providing a 1% return on investment daily. Refinery will never run out, as users mint their own rewards the longer they participate / reinvest. The more referrals a player gets, that player and their team can increase how fast they ROI on their initial deposits. Every direct referral by a player provides 10% income on deposits. An individual player can have an unlimited amount of direct referrals. Each direct referal becomes the head of a team chain, so a player can have an unlimited amount of team chains. This is true for EVERY player, a player has one and only one buddy/upline and many teams/downlines. Each team chain earns additional rewards as withdrawals are made from up to 15 players in the team/downline. A downline is a team of players linked by buddies/uplines. Each direct referral you have is the head of a downline in your team, so focus on organic referrals. Rewards in each generation of your downlines are as follows: 1st: 30%, 2nd - 5th: 10%, 6th - 10th: 8%, and 11th - 15th: 5%.
In addition every deposit, reinvest, and withdraw generates a 5% MYNTX buyback which is sent to Treasury investors. A minimum of 1 MYNTX must be deposited to get started.
