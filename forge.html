About

MYNT Forge is the official MYNTX liquidity pool on the network. A low transaction fee of 0.3% is charged on buys and sells of MYNTX against the pool. The fees are paid out exclusively to liquidity providers that participate via Stockpile. Forge is the preferred way to enter in and out of MYNTX positions as needed; ultimately players want to stake MYNTX in a farm for maximum long term gains over time.
